About GKHack

GKHack is the annual Hackathon hosted by Geekulcha that aims to put together a massive brain activity of our geeks in one place to create cool things that matter! If you want to Learn and Build cool things or make some new friends GKHack is the right event for you. We belive that our geeks can use their skills to solve real problems faced everyday, change people life and most important of all make AFRICA a better place.
